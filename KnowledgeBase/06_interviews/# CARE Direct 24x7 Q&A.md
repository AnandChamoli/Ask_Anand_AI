## Q: Tell me about CARE Direct 24x7.

A: CARE Direct 24x7 was an innovative customer support initiative designed for Powertrac and Farmtrac tractor owners. The solution enabled customers to connect directly with technical support through a dedicated CARE Button installed in tractors. The objective was to provide rapid assistance, improve service responsiveness, reduce downtime, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

## Q: What business problem did CARE Direct solve?

A: Farmers operating tractors in remote areas often faced challenges obtaining immediate technical assistance during critical agricultural operations. CARE Direct 24x7 addressed this problem by providing direct access to technical support, reducing response times, improving service accessibility, and minimizing equipment downtime.

---

## Q: How did the CARE Button work?

A: The CARE Button was installed directly in the tractor and connected through an integrated communication system. When a customer pressed the button, an alert was sent to a dedicated support center where trained technicians could immediately communicate with the tractor operator. The system enabled rapid assistance, complaint registration, and service coordination.
