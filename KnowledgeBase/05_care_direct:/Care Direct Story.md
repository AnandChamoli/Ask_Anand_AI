CARE Direct 24x7 - STAR Story
Situation
Farmers operating tractors in remote areas often faced challenges obtaining immediate technical assistance and service support. During peak agricultural seasons, equipment downtime could significantly impact productivity and customer satisfaction.
Task

Action
As Lead – Service Marketing at Escorts Kubota, I led the CARE Direct 24x7 initiative from concept to implementation.
Key responsibilities included:
	•	Defining the service concept and customer journey
	•	Developing the CARE Button prototype
	•	Integrating the solution with tractor platforms
	•	Establishing a multilingual customer support center
	•	Designing complaint management workflows
	•	Coordinating with dealers and service teams
	•	Creating service escalation processes
	•	Monitoring operational performance and customer experience
Result
	•	Introduced one of the first IoT-enabled customer support solutions in the Indian tractor industry
	•	Improved customer accessibility to service support
	•	Strengthened customer trust and satisfaction
	•	Enhanced service adherence and dealer responsiveness
	•	Supported growth of spare parts and lubricants business
