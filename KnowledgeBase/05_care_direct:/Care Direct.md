CARE Direct 24x7 – Customer Service Transformation Initiative
Company: Escorts Kubota Limited
Year: 2019
Role: Lead – Service Marketing
Project Type: Customer Experience Transformation | IoT Enablement | Service Operations | Business Transformation
Background
Indian tractor customers often operate in remote rural locations where access to technical support can be difficult. During peak agricultural seasons, tractor downtime can directly impact a farmer's livelihood.
Business Objective
Create an industry-leading customer support system that enables tractor owners to instantly connect with Escorts for technical assistance and service support.
Solution
Escorts launched the industry's first 24x7 Care Button across Powertrac and Farmtrac tractor ranges.
The solution included:
	•	Tractor-mounted Care Button
	•	Integrated SIM-enabled communication system
	•	Multilingual 24x7 call center
	•	Trained technical support engineers
	•	Dealer service escalation network
	•	End-to-end complaint management process
